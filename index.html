Daraven Perez

Born in Guam. Saved by Faith. Living for Christ.  

Born in Guam

On July 26, 2000, a child was born to two loving parents on the island of Guam. Weighing in at over 7 lbs., Daraven Michael Perez was welcomed into the world. Three years after his birth, Daraven grew stronger and healthier, and it was now time for him to begin pre-school. As a result, his mother enrolled him at Harvest Christian Academy, and he would eventually remain there until his Senior year of high school.

Saved by Faith

Growing up, Daraven went to a Christian school for all of his life and heard preaching each and every day. Yet, he never trusted in God to be His Savior. As years passed, Daraven knew that God existed, but he wanted to live his life for himself, seeking the joy and happiness that the lust of the eyes, the lust of the flesh, and the pride of life would supposedly bring. Continuing to live a life for himself, Daraven’s heart was overtaken by his sin, and there eventually came a time when he rejected God completely. However, God – in His love and mercy – allowed Daraven to experience difficulties and trials that made him realize that the life he was living was empty and hopeless. Then, during his Junior year of high school, God sent an evangelist to Harvest Christian Academy to speak for their chapel services. Sitting in the front pews, Daraven heard these messages and was convicted by the Holy Spirit. For the first time in his life, Daraven was able to hear the Gospel and realized that he was a sinner in need of Christ. At the end of the service, he repented of his sins to God and asked for forgiveness. On November 9, 2016, Daraven became a Christian, and his life was changed forever!

Living for Christ

After getting saved during his Junior year in high school, Daraven had a new desire to serve Christ for the rest of his life. Because of this, after graduation, he decided to go to Bob Jones University in order to receive high quality education in a Christian environment. Currently, he is majoring in Biblical Counseling and Pre-Physical Therapy and is excited for what God has in store for him. No matter what he ends up doing in life, Daraven will continue living for Christ, bringing honor and glory to God forever and ever.
